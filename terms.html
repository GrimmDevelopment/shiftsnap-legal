ShiftSnap is a shift-tracking and earnings-estimation application designed for hourly and shift workers. The App allows you to:

  • Record clock-in and clock-out times for work shifts
  • Estimate gross pay, overtime pay, and take-home pay based on rates and settings you configure
  • Log tips, expenses, notes, photos, videos, and reminders associated with your shifts
  • Track mileage and work locations using GPS (Premium feature)

  • Generate PDF timesheets and invoices for personal use (Premium feature)
  • Export shift data to CSV (Premium feature)
  • Access an AI assistant ("Shifty") for questions about your shift history and earnings (Premium feature)
