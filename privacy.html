1. Introduction & Who We Are

ShiftSnap is operated by Matthew Ryan Grimmett, an individual developer based in West Virginia, United States ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect through the ShiftSnap mobile application (the "App"), how we use it, with whom we share it, how we protect it, and the rights you have over it.

By downloading or using the App you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Contact: support.shiftsnap@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information

  • Full name (entered at sign-up or onboarding)
  • Email address (for email/password accounts)
  • Password — stored only as a cryptographic hash. We never store or see your plain-text password.
  • If you use Sign in with Apple and choose "Hide My Email," Apple provides a private relay address; we do not receive your real Apple ID or password.

Job and Work Configuration

  • Job name, employer name, icon, and color
  • Hourly rate, overtime thresholds, pay multipliers, per-diem settings, and travel reimbursement amounts
  • Expected shift duration; per-day shift schedules; pay schedule (frequency and next pay date)
  • State or region of employment
  • Workplace GPS coordinates (for arrival-alert geofencing — Pro feature)

Shift Records

  • Clock-in and clock-out timestamps (UTC); duration in seconds; break and lunch times
  • Gross pay, overtime pay, double-time pay, and estimated take-home pay
  • A snapshot of the hourly rate in effect at the time of the shift
  • Whether the entry was made manually (including a manual note)

Tips and Cash Payments

  • Amount, optional customer name, and optional description for each payment you log

Expenses

  • Amount, category, description, date, and the shift or job it belongs to
  • Receipt photos you choose to attach (uploaded to secure cloud storage)

Notes and Media (Pro)

  • Free-text notes you write, including voice-to-text transcripts
  • Photos, videos, and audio files you attach to shifts (uploaded to secure cloud storage)

Tax Settings

  • Federal, state/provincial, local, and payroll tax rates; filing status; custom flat rate (if configured)

Invoice Data (Pro)

When you generate an invoice you may enter your client's name, email address, and mailing address, as well as your own business name, email, and address. This information is stored in our database so you can retrieve and re-use invoices across sessions. Invoice PDF files are generated on your device and shared via your device's native share sheet — the PDF is not uploaded to our servers.

In-App Reminders

  • Text reminders you write — stored locally on your device only, never on our servers.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Location Data (only when you grant permission)

  • GPS at clock-in / clock-out (Pro): A single coordinate snapshot plus reverse-geocoded address recorded when you clock in or out.
  • Live shift map: Your device's GPS is sampled for real-time on-screen display during an active shift. These readings are not saved individually to our servers.
  • Manual waypoints: When you tap "Log Location" during a shift, your coordinates at that moment are saved to your shift record.
  • Mileage tracking (Pro): GPS position updates accumulate a driven-distance total. Only the final mileage total is stored; your route path is not stored.
  • Geofencing (Pro): You save a fixed workplace coordinate. iOS monitors your proximity in the background to trigger a local notification. We do not log entry/exit events — only the static center coordinate you saved is stored on our servers.

Error and Performance Data (via Sentry)

  • Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Error type, message, and stack trace (sent when a crash or exception occurs)
  • Performance traces for approximately 20% of app sessions (navigation timing, network call durations, console breadcrumbs)
  • Your user identifier and email address are tagged to Sentry events from sign-in so errors can be correlated to your account during debugging

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect biometric data. Face ID / Touch ID authentication is handled entirely by your device's OS; we receive only a pass/fail result.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers or track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not send your conversations with Shifty to any external AI service. All AI processing is handled on-device using Apple Foundation Models (iOS 26+) or a local deterministic rule engine. No third-party AI API receives your data.

5. Data Stored Only On Your Device

The following is stored locally on your device in encrypted app storage and is never uploaded to our servers:

  • Shifty (AI) conversation history — your chat messages and Shifty's replies (up to 100 messages), stored under a user-scoped key in your device's local app storage.
  • Widget and Live Activity state — your current shift status (job name, hourly rate, estimated earnings, break status) stored in iOS App Group shared storage for the Home Screen widget and Live Activity. Visible on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island to anyone who can see your device screen.
  • Offline action queue — clock-in and clock-out actions queued locally when there is no network connectivity, automatically replayed when connectivity returns.
  • In-app reminders — text reminders you set for yourself.
  • Cached data — temporary copies of recently loaded shift history, job lists, and your profile (7-day TTL).
  • Geofence cooldown timestamps — the last time an arrival alert fired for each job, to enforce the 2-hour notification cooldown.

7. Shifty — AI Assistant and Your Data

7.1 On-Device AI (iOS 26+ / Apple Intelligence)

On supported devices running iOS 26 or later with Apple Intelligence enabled, Shifty uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework. Your shift data and conversation never leave your device for AI processing.

7.2 Deterministic Fallback (All Other Devices)

On devices where Apple Intelligence is not available, Shifty uses a local rule-based pattern-matching engine running entirely on your device. No data leaves your device for AI processing.

Notice: Shifty's responses are for informational purposes only and do not constitute tax, legal, accounting, financial, or employment advice. Consult a qualified professional for any such decisions.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Active account: Data retained indefinitely to allow access to your full shift history.
  • After account deletion: All your records are deleted from our primary database immediately. Residual copies in encrypted backups are purged within 30 days.
  • Sentry error logs: Retained per Sentry's retention policy (typically 90 days).
  • Purchase records: Apple and RevenueCat retain transaction records for financial and legal compliance; we cannot delete these on their behalf.

How to Delete Your Data

  • Clear Cache (in-app): Settings → Clear Cache — removes locally stored conversation history, reminders, and cached data. Does not remove server-side data.
  • Delete Account (in-app): Settings → Delete Account — permanently and irreversibly deletes all your data from our database and storage. You will be immediately signed out. This cannot be undone.
  • Email request: Email support.shiftsnap@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request" and we will complete deletion within 30 days.

11. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures including:

  • All data in transit encrypted with HTTPS / TLS 1.2+
  • Authentication credentials stored in Supabase's secure auth system, hashed with bcrypt; session tokens stored in iOS Keychain
  • Media files stored in a private cloud storage bucket accessible only via time-limited signed URLs
  • Database access enforced by Row Level Security (RLS) policies — users can only read and write their own rows
No security system is impenetrable. Despite our measures, we cannot guarantee that unauthorized parties will never access, intercept, alter, or destroy your data. By using the App, you acknowledge and accept this inherent risk. We are not liable for damages resulting from a security breach that is not directly caused by our own gross negligence or willful misconduct.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a confirmed security incident affecting your personal information, we will notify you by email within the timeframe required by applicable law — typically within 72 hours of discovery for users in jurisdictions with mandatory breach notification laws (EEA, UK, Norway, Switzerland, Canada/Quebec, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, Nigeria, and others). Notification will describe the nature of the incident, the categories of data affected, and steps being taken to address it.
