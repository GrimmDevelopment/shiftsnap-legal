1. Introduction

ShiftSnap ("we," "us," or "our") operates the ShiftSnap mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information

  • Full name (provided at sign-up)
  • Email address (provided at sign-up or via Sign In with Apple)
  • Password (transmitted securely and stored only as a cryptographic hash — we never store your plain-text password)

If you sign in with Apple and choose "Hide My Email," Apple provides a private relay email address to us instead of your real email. We do not receive your Apple ID password.

Job and Work Information

  • Job name, job icon, and job color you configure
  • Hourly rate, overtime thresholds, and pay rate settings
  • Per diem / day rate settings
  • Expected shift duration
  • Pay schedule (frequency and next pay date)
  • State of employment

Shift Records

  • Clock-in and clock-out times and dates
  • Hours worked per shift
  • Gross pay, overtime pay, and estimated take-home pay
  • Break and lunch start/end times

Tips and Payments

  • Tip amounts you log, including optional customer name and description

Expenses

  • Expense amount, category, description, and date
  • Receipt photos you choose to attach to expense records

Notes and Media

  • Text notes you write for your shifts
  • Photos you take or choose from your photo library and attach to shift notes
  • Videos you record or choose and attach to shift notes

Reminders

  • Text reminders you write to yourself within the App

Tax Settings

  • Federal, state, local, and FICA tax rates you configure
  • Custom flat tax rate (if you choose to set one)

AI Conversation History

  • Questions you send to Shifty, our AI assistant, and Shifty's responses

Regional Preferences

  • Country and currency selection
  • Theme preference

Invoice Data (entered by you, not stored on our servers)

When you create an invoice using the Invoices feature, you may enter client information including client name, email address, and mailing address. You may also enter your own business name, email, and address. This information is used solely to populate the invoice PDF, which is generated on your device. Client information you enter for invoices is not transmitted to or stored on our servers — it exists only in the App's memory during the session and in the PDF file saved to your device.

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

Location Data (only when you grant permission)

  • GPS coordinates and accuracy at the time you clock in or clock out (when GPS logging is enabled)
  • Street address, city, state, and country derived from those coordinates via reverse geocoding
  • GPS coordinates you designate as a work location for geofencing (arrival alerts), stored as a fixed center point — we do not log your movement to or from that location
  • Real-time GPS position during active mileage trips — this is processed on-device and only the total distance driven is stored; your route is not recorded
  • Continuous GPS position updates while a shift is actively clocked in — your device's GPS is sampled approximately every 4 seconds or every 5 meters of movement to power the live shift map display. Individual position readings are not individually stored; this data drives the real-time map only
  • Manual waypoint ("Log Location") — when you press the Log Location button during a shift, your GPS coordinates at that moment are saved to your shift record as a waypoint entry

Device and Technical Information (via Sentry, our error and performance monitoring service)

  • Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Error type, message, and stack trace (sent when a crash or error occurs)
  • Performance traces — approximately 20% of all app sessions are sampled for performance monitoring, which may include breadcrumbs such as navigation events, network call timings, and console log entries
  • Your user identifier and email address, which are tagged on all Sentry events (errors and performance traces) from the moment you sign in, so we can correlate issues to your account during debugging

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect biometric data. Face ID and fingerprint authentication are processed entirely by your device's operating system; we receive only a success or failure result.
  • We do not collect audio recordings. Microphone permission is declared because video recording may capture audio, but we do not record or store audio independently.
  • Location is collected only when you explicitly enable GPS logging, mileage tracking, or geofencing — and only for the purposes described in this policy. When a shift is actively clocked in with the live map feature enabled, GPS is sampled continuously on-device for map display; this is not transmitted to our servers as individual position records.
  • We do not collect advertising identifiers or track you across other apps or websites.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate the App — create and manage your account, record your shifts, calculate earnings estimates, and provide all App features
  • Calculate estimates — estimate gross pay, overtime, take-home pay after estimated taxes, and earnings summaries based on your configured rates and tax settings
  • Generate documents — produce PDF timesheets and invoices on your device for your personal use
  • Power AI responses — provide Shifty with context about your shifts and earnings so it can answer your questions about your work history
  • Send local notifications — deliver clock-out reminders, payday reminders, and work-arrival alerts that you configure within the App
  • Sync across sessions — store your data securely in the cloud so it is available when you sign in on a new device or after reinstalling the App
  • Monitor for errors and performance — detect and fix crashes and bugs; a 20% sample of app sessions is monitored for performance issues using Sentry's performance tracing, which may include timing data for screens, navigation events, and network calls

5. Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers:

  • AI conversation history — your Shifty chat messages (last 100 messages), stored in your device's local app storage
  • Widget state — your current shift status (clocked in/out, break status, job name, hourly rate, and estimated gross earnings), stored in iOS App Groups shared storage for the Home Screen widget. This data is also displayed on your device's Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island. Lock Screen and Dynamic Island data is visible to anyone who can see your device without unlocking it. You can disable Live Activity from the Dynamic Island at any time in the App's settings.
  • Offline action queue — clock-in and clock-out actions queued while your device is offline, stored locally until connectivity is restored
  • Theme preference — your selected app theme (Dark, Light, Mono, Azure, System)
  • Cached data — temporary copies of recently loaded shift history and media URLs for faster loading

Clearing local data: You can clear locally cached data by going to Settings → Clear Cache within the App. This removes your AI conversation history, theme preferences, and cached shift data. It does not remove your authentication tokens, which are stored separately in your device's secure keychain (iOS Keychain) and are only removed when you sign out or delete your account.

6. Location Information

6.1 When We Collect Location

We collect location information only in the following specific circumstances, each of which requires your explicit permission:

  • GPS logging (optional, Premium feature): When enabled, we record your GPS coordinates and reverse-geocoded address when you clock in or clock out of a shift. You control this setting at Settings → GPS Logging.
  • Live shift map (when a shift is clocked in with GPS enabled): While a shift is actively in progress, your device's GPS is sampled approximately every 4 seconds or every 5 meters of movement to power the real-time map display inside the App. These continuous position readings are used only for the on-screen map and are not individually saved to our servers as location records. This runs only while the App is in the foreground during an active shift.
  • Manual waypoint logging: When you press the "Log Location" button during an active shift, your current GPS coordinates are recorded and saved to your shift record as a labeled waypoint entry. These waypoints are stored in your shift history on our servers.
  • Mileage tracking (optional, Premium feature): When you start a trip, your device's GPS tracks your position to calculate distance. Only the total mileage for the trip is saved — your route is not recorded.
  • Geofencing / Arrival alerts (optional, Premium feature): You designate a fixed work location coordinate. Your device's operating system monitors your approximate proximity to that location in the background. We do not log when you enter or exit the geofence area — we only send a local notification prompting you to clock in.

6.2 Background Location

Background location access ("Always" permission on iOS) is used exclusively for geofencing. We do not use background location for any other purpose.

6.3 Map Tiles

When displaying a map showing your clock-in location or a live map during your shift, the map tiles (the visual map images) are fetched from Apple Maps (iOS) or Google Maps (Android). This is standard map usage subject to those providers' privacy policies.

7. AI Features and Data

7.1 On-Device AI (Apple Intelligence)

On supported devices running iOS 26 or later with Apple Intelligence enabled, Shifty uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models. Your data does not leave your device for this feature.

7.2 Deterministic Fallback

For devices where Apple Intelligence is not available, Shifty uses a local pattern-matching system to answer questions about your shift data. No data leaves your device.

7.3 Cloud AI Fallback (Anthropic)

In limited circumstances where neither on-device AI path can answer your question, your question and a summary of your shift data (earnings, hours, job names) may be sent to an Anthropic API via our secure server-side function. This data is used solely to generate a response to your question. We do not send your name, email, or account credentials to Anthropic.

AI responses from Shifty are for informational purposes only and do not constitute tax, legal, accounting, financial, or employment advice. You should consult a qualified professional for advice on your specific situation.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information from our systems, subject to legal retention obligations.

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Clear Cache (Settings): Removes locally stored data (conversation history, preferences, cached files). Your cloud data is not affected.
  • Clear All Data (Settings): Permanently deletes all your shifts, jobs, notes, photos, videos, expenses, tips, and location records from our cloud database. Your account login is preserved but your data is permanently removed.
  • Delete Account (Settings): Permanently deletes all your data AND your account. This is irreversible.

Deleted data may remain in our backups for up to 30 days before being purged from backup systems.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
  • Media files are stored in a private cloud storage bucket and accessed only via time-limited signed URLs
  • Database access is restricted by Row Level Security policies that ensure users can only access their own data
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text — only a cryptographic hash is retained

No security system is impenetrable. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your device software up to date.
