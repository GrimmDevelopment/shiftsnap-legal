Privacy Policy

Effective: May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Matthew Ryan Grimmett, an individual operating in West Virginia, United States ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the ShiftSnap mobile application (the "App"). We respect your privacy and only collect the minimum data needed for the App to function.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign up, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Full name (if you choose to provide it, or via Sign in with Apple)
  • An encrypted password (handled by Supabase Auth — we never see plaintext passwords)

1.2 Work & Shift Data

The App stores everything you create inside it:

  • Jobs (name, employer, hourly rate, color, icon)
  • Shifts (clock-in and clock-out times, hours worked, breaks)
  • Notes (text you type during a shift)
  • Photos and videos you attach to shifts
  • Tax settings (federal / state / local / FICA rates you configure)
  • Pay schedule (period type and next pay date)

1.3 Location Data (optional, Pro feature)

If you enable GPS Location Logging in Settings, the App records your device's latitude and longitude when you tap "Log Location" or clock in. Location is never recorded passively in the background — only when you take an action that requests it. You can disable GPS logging at any time in Settings → Premium Tools → GPS Logging.

1.4 Subscription & Purchase Data

Subscription status is managed by Apple In-App Purchase and RevenueCat. We receive the entitlement status ("Pro" vs. "Free"), product identifier, renewal date, and an anonymous user identifier — but we do not see your credit card or full Apple ID. All payment processing is handled by Apple.

1.5 Diagnostic Data

We may receive crash reports and basic diagnostic information from Expo and Apple to fix bugs. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To run the App. Your shifts, notes, photos, and settings power every feature you see.
  • To sync across devices. Pro users get cloud sync so the same data appears on every device they sign in to.
  • To manage subscriptions. RevenueCat confirms whether you have an active subscription.
  • To improve the App. Aggregated crash data helps us fix bugs.
  • To contact you. If you email us at our support address, we'll use your email to reply.

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with data brokers.

4. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, all your data is removed from our active databases within 30 days. Backups are rotated and fully overwritten within 90 days.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Export your data (PDF timesheets and invoices are available inside the App).
  • Object to processing or withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email shiftsnapsupport@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

ShiftSnap is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

All traffic between the App and our backend uses HTTPS encryption. Passwords are hashed with industry-standard algorithms by Supabase. Data at rest is encrypted on Supabase's servers. That said, no system is 100% secure — if you suspect your account has been compromised, change your password immediately and email us.

8. International Users

The App is operated from the United States. If you use the App from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. By using the App you consent to that transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced inside the App. The "Effective" date at the top of this page indicates when the latest version took effect.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your data? Email shiftsnapsupport@gmail.com.
