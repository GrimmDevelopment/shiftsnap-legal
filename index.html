Account & Data

To delete your account and all associated data, open ShiftSnap and go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your shifts, jobs, and personal information from our servers.

To clear locally cached data only (conversation history, preferences), go to Settings → Clear Cache.

Subscriptions

ShiftSnap Pro subscriptions are managed entirely through the Apple App Store. To cancel, update, or view your subscription, go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → ShiftSnap.

For refund requests, please use Apple's standard App Store refund process.

Frequently Asked Questions

My clock-in time looks wrong. If you clocked in while offline, the timestamp syncs when connectivity is restored. The final record uses the original clock-in time, not the sync time.

My widget isn't updating. iOS controls how often widgets refresh. If your widget appears stale, open the app briefly to force a sync. Live Activities update in real time when the app is active.

I can't find Shifty AI. Shifty requires iOS 26 or later with Apple Intelligence enabled for the full on-device AI experience. It's available from the chat icon inside the app.
